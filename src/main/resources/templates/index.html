About Us

A Tool and Die Manufacturing Company specializes in designing, creating, and maintaining precision tools, dies, molds, and machinery used in manufacturing processes. These tools are essential for shaping, cutting, and forming materials like metal, plastic, or composites to produce parts for various industries, including automotive, aerospace, electronics, and consumer goods.

Our Vision

To develop and become a nationally acclaimed Unit of Excellence in the area of Design, Development and Manufacturing of Quality Tools, Moulds, Machining Components, Jigs and Fixtures and Skill development.

Our Mission

To continuously improve the skills in design, development and manufacturing processes of Quality Tools and Components. And to optimize design and manufacturing using skilled labour and latest Methods and Techniques to meet and satisfy your customer needs.

Business Type

Designers and Manufacturers of Press Tools, Moulds, Dies, Gauges, Precision Components, Machine Parts, Welding Fixtures, Machining Fixtures and Jigs. Traders of standard components, press tool and mould accessories.
